What this demo shows: Traditional rule-based fraud systems (used by most cooperative banks today) rely on fixed thresholds — e.g. "flag any transaction above ₹50,000 or after 11pm." AI models learn the behavioural envelope of each customer and detect anomalies even when individual signals don't cross any fixed threshold. The gap between the two is measured in fraud rupees missed and legitimate customers wrongly blocked.
